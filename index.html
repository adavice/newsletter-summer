Monica’s Tips & Tricks ☕

Monica

Helpful lifehacks and work organization ideas
Smart tips for staying sane and getting things done:

1. Focus 3 Rule

Start your day by choosing just 3 key tasks.

2. Time-Blocking > To-Do Lists

Structure your day in blocks instead of endless lists.

3. Templates Are Magic

Automate repetitive tasks with message or report templates.

4. 20-20-20 Rule

Every 20 minutes, look 20 feet away for 20 seconds.

5. Friday Wrap-Up Ritual

Spend 15 minutes on Friday closing tasks and prepping next week.
